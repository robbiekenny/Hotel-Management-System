User Manual

Start Screen

    1. Number of nights: Choose the number of nights you wish to stay here
    2. Month: Choose the month in which you want to stay in the hotel here
    3. Year: Choose the year in which you want to stay in the hotel here
    4. Calendar: When you press this calendar icon it will give you the option of choosing a particular day, month and year
    5. Arrival Date: Choose the date in which you want to stay in the hotel here
    6. Login Button: Once your username and password are entered, press the login button to enter the system
    7. Search button: Press the search button to bring up available rooms in relation to the dates picked
    8. Help Link: Click help to access our user manual
