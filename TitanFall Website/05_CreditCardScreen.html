Credit Card Screen

    1. Choose a credit card type
    2. Insert a valid credit card number
    3. Insert the 3 digit secuirty code at the back of the credit card
    4. Insert the expiry date from the credit card
    5. Insert your name from the credit card
    6. Cancel Button: Press the cancel button to bring you back to the user home screen
    7. Pay Now Button: Press the pay now button to confirm the booking
    Your credit card information is not kept in our system for security purposes
